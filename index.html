My Name is Sam Vargas... this is My Story

I was born a first generation immigrant: my father came from Peru. I grew up reading Marvel and DC comics, and the futuristic technology used by superheroes got me interested in ‘engineering’ and making things.

I joined my middle school team’s VEX robotics club and learned about real robots for the first time. I became very good at building and designing the robot, and was also interested in the coding process.

In highschool, I got exposed to a 'Makerspace', which had all the tools that I'd always wanted but never could have to make things. Throughout my early highschool years I made many fun projects with my friends. I later used the Makerspace to start my highschool's VEX robotics team, and got over 20 people to join the club.

Around the same time, I started a non-profit organization called AV Designs with my friend. We both really enjoyed CAD and 3D designing, and we're asked to design and 3D print supplies that a non-profit organization needed. After many successful projects with them, we started our non-profit and began servicing other non-profits.

Because of all the work I had been doing in the technology and engineering field, my Computer Science teacher asked me to apply for an internship called the Catapult Program. I applied in 2023 and got accepted, but due to conflicting times was not able to participate until the summer of 2024.
